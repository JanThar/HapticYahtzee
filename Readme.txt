A haptic Yahtzee scorecard

Can be printed either in one or two colors. 
Afterwards glue in 2.5mm washers (test beforehand if magnets hold on them) - be aware that there exist different outer diameter
5mm magnets are then used as sort of haptic checkboxes.
For other languages than german change strings at the beginning, most interesting might be finding the correct braille font to include in OpenScad.
